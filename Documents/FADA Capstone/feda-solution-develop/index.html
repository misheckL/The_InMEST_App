OUR SOLUTION

The high costs and time-consuming nature of creating, administering and evaluating assessments in universities is straining resources and increasing faculty workload. This is what Fedasol is here to solve. Our solution includes the following features!

  • Adaptive Testing: We have Implemented adaptive testing algorithms that adjust the difficulty of questions based on the student's previous responses, providing a more accurate measure of their abilities.
  • Question Bank: Our solution allows you to create a repository of questions that educators can use to build assessments. Including categorization and tagging options for easy search and selection.
  • Auto-Generation of Questions: Enable the tool to generate new questions based on predefined criteria, such as difficulty level, topic, or learning objectives.

We have several other features that are tailored towards meeting our clients needs.
